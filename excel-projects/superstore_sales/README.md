## Global Superstore Sales & Profit Dashboard (Excel)
### Project Overview

This project analyzes global sales data from a fictional Global Superstore from [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/fatihilhan/global-superstore-dataset/data) using Microsoft Excel. The goal was to explore sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends across different markets and time periods, and present the results in a clear, interactive dashboard.

The project focuses on analysis using PivotTables, calculated fields, slicers, and clean visual design.

### Dataset

Source: Kaggle – Global Superstore Dataset
Time Period: 2011–2014
Scope: Global sales across multiple markets, regions, customer segments, and product categories.

Key columns include:
- Sales, Profit, Discount, Quantity
- Order Date, Ship Date, Year, Week Number
- Market, Region, Country, City
- Category, Sub‑Category, Product
- Customer Segment
 
### Dashboard Features

The Excel dashboard includes:
- KPIs: Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, Total Orders
- Trend Analysis: Monthly Sales & Profit over time
- Market Analysis: Sales vs Profit by Market
- Customer Analysis: Profit by Customer Segment
- Product Analysis: Top & Bottom Sub‑Categories by Profit
- Interactive Slicers: Year, Market, Segment, Category
All charts update dynamically based on slicer selections.

### Summary

Sales and profit vary significantly by market and product type.
- A small number of sub‑categories generate most of the profit.
- Some products consistently underperform and contribute very little profit.
- Interactive filters allow deeper exploration of trends by year and segment.
